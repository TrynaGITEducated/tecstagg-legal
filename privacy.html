Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

RUBRIX ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

Files You Upload: When you use RUBRIX, you upload assignment and rubric files. These files are processed temporarily and are not permanently stored after analysis.

Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage data (e.g., number of analyses per day, file types) to improve our service.

Session Information: We use session cookies to track your free tier usage and Pro status. No personal information is stored in these sessions.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide AI-powered assignment analysis
  • To improve and optimize our service
  • To enforce our free tier limits
  • To detect and prevent abuse

3. Data Storage and Security

We process your uploaded files in real-time using Google's Gemini API. Files are not permanently stored on our servers. Analysis results are displayed immediately and not retained unless you save them manually.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini API: For AI analysis of your assignments
  • Flask Session: For temporary session management

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of any data we may have stored
  • Opt out of usage analytics
  • Access any personal information we hold

6. Children's Privacy

RUBRIX is intended for use by educators and students over the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes via email or through the RUBRIX website.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@rubrix.com
